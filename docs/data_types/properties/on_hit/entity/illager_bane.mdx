# Illager Bane Property

## Description

  
The Illager Bane Property boosts damage dealt to entities classified as Illagers or their allies. This includes Illagers and their associated friends, providing enhanced effectiveness against these specific entity types.This property is particularly useful in scenarios where players need to deal additional damage to Illager-type mobs, such as during raids or in areas populated with these enemies.

## Data

- **illager_bane**:  the amount of damage increase.
